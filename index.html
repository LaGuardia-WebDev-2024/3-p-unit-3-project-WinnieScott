Mashed Potatoes

ingredient amount
potatoes 3 pounds
garlic 3 cloves
unsalted butter half a cup
milk one cup
salt one and a half teaspoons
pepper as much as you want
  • add potatoes, garlic, and salt to a pot
  • boil for about 15 minutes
  • drain potatoes in to a colander
  • mash potatoes and add the rest of ingredients

Roasted Potatoes

ingredient amount
potatoes 2 pounds
olive oil 2 tablespoons
garlic powder 1 tablespoon
chopped fresh herbs 3 tablespoons
paprika 1/2 teaspoon
salt as much as you want
pepper as much as you want
  • preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit
  • cut potatoes in to 1 inch cubes
  • mix with all other ingredients
  • spread on a baking sheet and roast for 30 minutes

Baked sweet potato fries

ingredient amount
sweet potatoes 2 pounds
cornstarch 1 tablespoon
salt 1/2 teaspoon
olive oil 2 tablespoons
pepper as much as you want
  • preheat oven to 425 degrees
  • put parchment paper on pans
  • peel and cut fries in to about 1/4 inch thick pieces
  • toss fries in corstarch, salt, and oil
  • disperse evenly on pans
  • bake for 20 minutes, flip, then bake for 15 more minutes
  • season
